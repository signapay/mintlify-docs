title: "Financial Reporting Overview"
description: "Residuals, deposits, fees, and statement data"
---

The Financial Reporting endpoints expose read-only monetary and transaction data used by finance teams, operations staff, and merchant-facing UIs.

## What's included

### Deposits

- **Deposits** — List and retrieve daily or batch funding records.
- **Deposit details** — Line-item breakdown of each deposit record.

### Chargebacks

- **Chargebacks** — List and retrieve dispute records tied to merchant transactions.
- **Related chargebacks** — Retrieve other chargebacks related to a given dispute.

### Residuals

- **ISO residual summaries** — Historical residual payout periods with aggregate metrics.
- **Residual metrics** — Aggregated KPI metrics across all residual periods.
- **Gross details** — Cross-residual gross fee breakdown.
- **Merchant summaries** — Per-merchant residual breakdowns within a residual period, also queryable across all periods.
- **Merchant details** — Fee category line items for a merchant's residual summary.
- **Overrides** — Manual residual adjustments for a period, also queryable across all periods.

### Transactions

- **Transactions** — List and retrieve individual card transactions with filtering. Look up by external reference ID.
- **Transaction notes** — Internal notes attached to a transaction.

### Transaction batches

- **Batches** — List and retrieve settlement batch records.
- **Batch notes** — Internal notes on a batch.

### Statements

- **Statements** — List and retrieve merchant or partner statement records.
- **Statement documents** — Download a document attached to a statement.
- **Statement notes** — Internal notes on a statement.
