---
title: "Addresses Overview"
description: "Manage business addresses on an application record"
---

Business addresses capture the physical location(s) associated with a merchant application, including the primary place of business and any additional locations required for underwriting.

| Endpoint | Description |
| --- | --- |
| [List addresses](/api-reference/leads/list-lead-addresses) | Retrieve all business addresses for an application |
| [Add address](/api-reference/leads/add-lead-address) | Add a business address to an application |
| [Get address](/api-reference/leads/get-lead-address) | Retrieve a single business address |
| [Update address](/api-reference/leads/update-lead-address) | Update a business address |
