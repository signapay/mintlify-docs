---
title: "Overview"
description: "Manage merchant applications through the full underwriting and boarding workflow"
---

Applications (leads) capture everything needed to underwrite and board a new merchant — business details, ownership, pricing, equipment, and e-signatures. Once approved, a lead is transitioned into an active merchant record.
