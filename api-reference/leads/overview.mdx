---
title: "Overview"
description: "Manage prospective merchant leads through the sales pipeline"
---

Leads represent merchant applications in progress. A lead captures everything needed to underwrite and board a new merchant — business details, ownership, pricing, equipment, and e-signatures. Once approved, a lead is transitioned into an active merchant record.

## Lifecycle

Leads move through a configurable pipeline of statuses:

- Use [Transition status](/api-reference/leads/transition-lead-status) to move a lead to a new pipeline stage.
- Use [Submit for review](/api-reference/leads/submit-lead-for-review) to send a lead to underwriting or compliance.
