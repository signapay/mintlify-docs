Leads represent merchant applications in progress. A lead captures everything needed to underwrite and board a new merchant — business details, ownership, pricing, equipment, and e-signatures. Once approved, a lead is transitioned into an active merchant record.

## Lifecycle

Leads move through a configurable pipeline of statuses:

- Use `PUT /leads/{leadId}/transition/{status}` to move a lead to a new status.
- Use `PUT /leads/{leadId}/submit/{reviewer}` to send a lead to underwriting or compliance for review.

## What's included

### Core

- **Lead CRUD** — Create, list, retrieve, update, and delete leads.
- **MID** — Retrieve the assigned merchant identifier once boarding is complete.
- **Risk profile** — Update the lead's risk classification.
- **Service acceptance** — Retrieve and update service terms acceptance.
- **Sensitive data** — Decrypt protected fields (SSN, bank account, etc.) on demand.

### Lead sources

- **Lead sources** — Create and manage referral and campaign sources. Associate or remove sources on individual leads.

### Business profile

- **Business addresses** — Add and manage multiple business location addresses.
- **Principals** — Capture beneficial owners and signers with encrypted SSN and date-of-birth support.
- **Deposit info** — ACH funding account details with field-level decryption.

### Documents & notes

- **Documents** — Attach, retrieve, update, and delete supporting documents.
- **Notes** — Add internal notes for team collaboration.

### Pricing

- **Pricing items** — Individual fee line items (e.g. interchange, basis points, per-item fees).
- **Pricing packages** — Grouped pricing configurations.
- **Pricing rules** — Conditional pricing logic applied to the lead.

### Equipment

- **Equipment models** — Define the terminal hardware associated with the lead.
- **Equipment orders** — Create and manage deployment orders.
- **Order items** — Line items within an equipment order.
- **Order gateway** — Gateway configuration for the equipment order.
- **Order file build type** — File build type for the equipment deployment.

### Compliance & validation

- **Completeness check** — Validate the lead against all required fields before submission.
- **DataX** — Identity verification.
- **Equifax** — Credit report and state ID verification.
- **GIACT** — Bank account validation.
- **MATCH Pro** — Mastercard terminated merchant screening.
- **TinCheck** — Tax ID verification.

### Signing

- **E-signature sessions** — Create and verify signing sessions for MPA agreement delivery.

### Clear identity verification

- **Clear sessions** — Initiate a Clear identity verification session.
- **Verification status** — Retrieve the result of a Clear verification.

### Fast Apply

Streamlined public-facing MPA form flow:

- **General** — Update the general application section.
- **Business** — Update the business details section.
- **Principals** — Save, create, and update principal records via the Fast Apply form.
