---
title: "Principals Overview"
description: "Manage beneficial owners and signatories on a lead record"
---

Principals are the individuals who own, control, or are responsible for the business applying for merchant processing. Each principal record captures personal identity details used for KYC and compliance screening, with sensitive fields stored encrypted.

| Endpoint | Description |
| --- | --- |
| [List principals](/api-reference/leads/list-lead-principals) | Retrieve all principals associated with a lead |
| [Add principal](/api-reference/leads/add-lead-principal) | Add a beneficial owner or signer to a lead |
| [Get principal](/api-reference/leads/get-lead-principal) | Retrieve a single principal by ID |
| [Update principal](/api-reference/leads/update-lead-principal) | Update a lead principal's information |
| [Delete principal](/api-reference/leads/delete-lead-principal) | Remove a principal from a lead record |
| [Decrypt principal data](/api-reference/leads/decrypt-lead-principal) | Decrypt a sensitive field on a principal |
