Merchants are fully boarded processing accounts linked to a client record. They have completed onboarding and are actively processing card transactions.

## What's included

### Core

- **Merchant records** — List, retrieve, and update merchant profiles.
- **Status transitions** — Activate, deactivate, or close a merchant via `PUT /merchants/{merchantId}/{action}`.
- **MID** — Retrieve the merchant identifier.
- **Risk profile** — Update the merchant's risk classification.
- **Service acceptance** — Retrieve and update service terms acceptance.
- **Terminal setup** — Retrieve and update terminal configuration. Download the VAR sheet PDF for deployment.
- **QA review** — Mark a merchant's quality assurance review as complete.
- **Sensitive data** — Decrypt protected fields on demand.

### Business profile

- **Business addresses** — Manage merchant location addresses.
- **Principals** — Add and update beneficial owners with encrypted sensitive field support.

### Documents & notes

- **Documents** — Attach, retrieve, update, and delete merchant compliance and onboarding documents.
- **Notes** — Internal notes on the merchant record.

### Pricing

- **Pricing items** — Individual fee line items.
- **Pricing packages** — Grouped pricing configurations.
- **Pricing templates** — Reusable pricing structures.
- **Pricing rules** — Conditional pricing logic.

### Equipment

- **Equipment models** — Terminal hardware records for the merchant.
- **Equipment orders** — Deployment orders with line items, gateway configuration, and file build type.

### ISO package

- **ISO package** — Create, retrieve, and remove the merchant's ISO package association.
