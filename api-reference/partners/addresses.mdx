---
title: "Addresses Overview"
description: "Manage business addresses on a partner record"
---

Business addresses record the physical location(s) associated with a partner organization, used for correspondence and compliance purposes.

| Endpoint | Description |
| --- | --- |
| [List addresses](/api-reference/partners/list-partner-addresses) | Retrieve all business addresses for a partner |
| [Add address](/api-reference/partners/add-partner-address) | Add a business address to a partner |
| [Get address](/api-reference/partners/get-partner-address) | Retrieve a single business address |
| [Update address](/api-reference/partners/update-partner-address) | Update a business address |
