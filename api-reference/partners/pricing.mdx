---
title: "Pricing Overview"
description: "Manage pricing items, packages, templates, and rules for a partner"
---

Partner pricing defines the fee structure that downstream merchants will inherit. Items define individual fee line items, packages group items into bundles, templates provide reusable configurations, and rules define conditional fee logic.

## Pricing items

| Endpoint | Description |
| --- | --- |
| [List pricing items](/api-reference/partners/list-partner-pricing-items) | Retrieve all pricing items for a partner |
| [Create pricing item](/api-reference/partners/create-partner-pricing-item) | Add a pricing item to a partner |
| [Get pricing item](/api-reference/partners/get-partner-pricing-item) | Retrieve a single pricing item |
| [Update pricing item](/api-reference/partners/update-partner-pricing-item) | Update a partner pricing item |
| [Delete pricing item](/api-reference/partners/delete-partner-pricing-item) | Remove a pricing item from a partner |
